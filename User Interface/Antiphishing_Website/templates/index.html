What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers attempt to trick you into revealing personal information, such as usernames, passwords, credit card details, or other sensitive data. These attacks often come in the form of fraudulent emails, text messages, or websites that appear to be legitimate but are designed to steal your data.

How to Protect Yourself?

  • Always verify the sender of an email before clicking on links or opening attachments.
  • Be cautious when visiting unfamiliar websites and avoid entering personal information on them.
  • Check for "https://" and a padlock icon in the browser's address bar when providing sensitive information online.
  • Use a robust email filtering system and keep your system's security software up-to-date.
  • When in doubt, contact the company or person directly using known contact methods.
